About Tranship Logistics

Tranship Logistics is a dynamic, family-owned logistics and supply chain solutions provider headquartered in Lagos, Nigeria. With a strong foundation in operational excellence and customer-centric service, we deliver fast, secure, and cost-effective logistics solutions across Nigeria, the African continent, and beyond.

Our mission is to empower businesses—particularly small and medium enterprises (SMEs)—by simplifying complex logistics challenges and enabling seamless trade in today’s global marketplace. We go beyond traditional transportation: we offer end-to-end supply chain support, including same-day urban delivery, reliable interstate and international shipping, customized bulk logistics, and secure escrow services that protect both buyers and sellers during cross-border transactions.

At Tranship, reliability is our cornerstone. We combine experienced professionals, real-time tracking technology, and a commitment to transparency to ensure every shipment arrives on time, in perfect condition, and with complete peace of mind. Whether you’re scaling your e-commerce operations, importing/exporting goods, or expanding into new markets, Tranship serves as your trusted strategic partner.

Our vision is to become the most dependable and innovative logistics provider in Africa and a preferred choice for international businesses seeking reliable entry into the African market. With Tranship, you gain not just a service provider, but a long-term ally dedicated to your success.

We invite you to experience the Tranship difference—where professionalism meets genuine care, and every delivery moves your business forward.
